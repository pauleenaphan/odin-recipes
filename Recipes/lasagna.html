Lasagna

The lasagna above has been in the family business for multiple generations. The sauce is made by my great great great great grandma.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta add salt to water
  2. Dice Tomatoes
  3. Mash and mix ground beef with black pepper
  4. Put one layer of pasta then tomato sauce then ground beef
  5. Repeat until reaching the top of the baking pan
