Chicken Alfredo

Chicken alfredo is so good it is probably one of my favorite dishes. I stole this recipe online for you so you can make some yourself.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Chicken Breast
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta noodles with salted water
  2. Season and cook chicken breast
  3. Add alfredo sauce pasta noodles and chicken
  4. Stir until desired consistency
